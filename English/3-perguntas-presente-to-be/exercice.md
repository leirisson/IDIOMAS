## Exercise 4 — Complete with the correct question word
*(what / who / where / when / why / how / which)*

2. _____ is she? → She is my teacher.
3. _____ is he? → He is in the kitchen.
4. _____ are they sad? → Because they are tired.
5. _____ is it ready? → It is ready at noon.
6. _____ is your bag? → The blue one.
