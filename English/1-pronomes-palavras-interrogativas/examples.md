# Personal Pronouns + Interrogative Words

## Pronouns
| Pronoun | Meaning  |
|---------|----------|
| I       | eu       |
| you     | você     |
| he      | ele      |
| she     | ela      |
| it      | isso/ele/ela (coisas/animais) |
| we      | nós      |
| they    | eles/elas |

## Interrogative Words
| Word  | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| what  | o que / qual |
| who   | quem   |
| where | onde   |
| when  | quando |
| why   | por que |
| how   | como   |
| which | qual   |

---

## Examples

### WHAT (o que / qual)
- What I need
- What you want
- What he thinks
- What she knows
- What it means
- What we have
- What they eat

### WHO (quem)
- Who I am
- Who you know
- Who he likes
- Who she calls
- Who we trust
- Who they follow

### WHERE (onde)
- Where I live
- Where you work
- Where he goes
- Where she stays
- Where it happens
- Where we meet
- Where they sleep

### WHEN (quando)
- When I arrive
- When you leave
- When he comes
- When she visits
- When it starts
- When we travel
- When they eat

### WHY (por que)
- Why I study
- Why you cry
- Why he runs
- Why she laughs
- Why it matters
- Why we try
- Why they stay

### HOW (como)
- How I learn
- How you feel
- How he reacts
- How she works
- How it sounds
- How we connect
- How they communicate

### WHICH (qual)
- Which I prefer
- Which you choose
- Which he picks
- Which she uses
- Which we need
- Which they select
