# Exercises — Verb To Be

---

## Exercise 1 — Fill in the blank with the correct form (am / is / are)

1. I _am_ happy. 
# Eu estou Feliz
2. She _is_ my sister.
# Ela é minha irmã
3. They _are_ at school.
# Eles estão na escola
4. He _is_ tall.
# Ele é alto
5. We _are_ friends.
# nós somos amigos
6. It _is_ cold today.
# hoje está frio
7. You _are_ a good student.
# você é um bom estudante
8. I _am_ not tired.
# eu não estou cansado
9. He _is_ not here.
# Ele não está aqui
10. They _are_ not ready.
# eles não estão prontos

## Exercise 3 — Turn into a question

1. You are okay. → are you okay ?
2. She is ready. → Is she ready ?
3. They are here. → Are they here ?
4. He is busy. → is he busy ?
5. It is possible. → Is it possible ?
6. We are late. → are we late ?
7. I am right. → am I right ?
